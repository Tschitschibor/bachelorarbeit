\begin{lemma}[$\sigma$-Lemma]
For each endomorphism $f$ in a finite concrete category $\mathcal{C}$ there exist $m,n\in\mathbb{N}$
such that $f^{(m+n)}=f^{m}$.
\end{lemma}
